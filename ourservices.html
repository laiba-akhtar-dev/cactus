A WORLD OF OPPORTUNITIES

We believe in the power of collaboration to open up opportunities for ourselves, our clients and our communities. With incredible growth and diversity, the UAE holds a unique position that invites ambition and creativity.

As such, we take our services a step further by providing set up and consultancy services to clients interested in moving into the UAE.

Focusing on tobacco and e-cigarette markets, we provide comprehensive and strategic support to ensure your success within the UAE and beyond.

Our long expertise and knowledge of UAE market, especially the tobacco industry serves as a catalyst to help similar business find their footing in the country and cater to the sophisticated requirements of audiences in the UAE. In the UAE alone, e-cigarette market value is projected to increase by 15.8% to reach US$78 million.

Our team of expert consultants provide a comprehensive service that covers all the requirements of setting up in the UAE from choosing a trade licence to setting up an office. As distributors, we also set up operation and logistics for our clients to hit the ground running.

  • Trade Licence Application
  • Government Approvals and Permits
  • Residency & UAE ID Application
  • Bank Accounts & Office Rentals
  • Product Registration
  • HR Management and Immigration Applications
  • Logistics & Operatio
  • VAT Application & Set Up
  • SKU & POS Set Up
  • Warehouse & Stock Management
